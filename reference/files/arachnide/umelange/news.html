14. September 2000  -  A new name
knoxos
    Ultimate Melange has been official renamed!
    Well nobody called it the long way either

    Simply UMelange will be the new name.


    By the way, I was sometimes asked:
    I know what "Ultimate" means, but what in heavens sake is "Melange" ?

    Well Melange (pronounced "m∆ lawn j") is a vienna's special sort of coffee:
    Recipie: Half coffee and half milk with a frothy crown

    I also recently discovered that the word is originally french and simply means "blend".
    Well wasn't supposed to, but maybe the modular concept of the project may fit in.
